SR-22 Basics

A filing requirement should be managed carefully, not guessed through.

Who May Need It

Drivers with no valid insurance, no valid license, multiple infractions, or at-fault accidents may face an SR-22 requirement.

Three-year Period

Many drivers must maintain SR-22 coverage for three years with no lapse in protection.

Avoid Lapses

Failing to maintain continuous coverage can lead to further penalties.

Owner SR-22

If you own or finance a vehicle, you may need an owner policy even if the vehicle is not paid off.

Non-owner SR-22

If you drive vehicles you do not own, a non-owner filing may be the right option.

Coverage Add-ons

Policies may be paired with collision, comprehensive, and uninsured motorist options when appropriate.
